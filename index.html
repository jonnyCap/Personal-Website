About me

My Name is Jonathan Maier and I am a Software Developer. I was born on 18th of November in 2002, thus I am almost 20 years old and currently studying "Software Engineering and Management" at the Technical University Graz.

I attended an heavily science based High School where contradictorily my interest in Software Engineering was developed. However, my School did not have great Offerings to get into Informatics, so on may way of self-learning I picked up the basics myself. I quickly realized how incomprehensible large the field of Computer Science was and I had a truly tough time to get into it.

As the years passed however, I was lucky to meet awesome People who shared their interests and knowledge with me, so I could get a superficial impression of what was going on in the amazing Aria of Computer Science and till this day I continue to learn and try new Things. However, I am still at the beginning of my journey!
