About me

- My traits, Resume and CV

Hello!

I'm Jonathan, a future Softwaredeveloper!
I want to show you my rather short Coding journy so far and tell you a bit about my self...

Highscool

I originally attended a high school specialising in natural sciences based in Austria, Graz. Fortunatly we had a few opportunity adjust our school timetable and from the 5th grade on I could focus on Informatics. We could choose specific courses, allthough some were not voluntary. However, that's were i got my first insights into Webdevelopment, ECDL, Game-Programming, Network-technologie as well as Software- and Hardware technologie.

Transition to Armed Forces

I apllied for a special IT-Eduction at the Armed Forces where only very few people got accepted. Thankfully i got accepted but since i head only little experience I felt like I had to prepare myself. In the course of my preperation I wanted to start learning how to code properly. So far I only programmed in a Programming-Language called "Programming" which is a JavaScript-API and wanted try out something quit different. So I got into Java, learned basic as well as advanced concepts and from there on I just programmed as much as possible to improve my Skills.

Austrian Armed Forces

With my solid 19 years I had to join the Austrian Armed Forces to complete my basic military service. As mentioned before, I was selected to be one of the few austrian "Cyber-Soldiers". At first we got special education which took 2 Month. During this time we firstly learned a lot about the military, its morals, traditions and so on. And secondly we got insights into subjects like Cryptography or the safe-military-network. Afterwards we were spread all over Austria again und worked practically for 4 Month. In that time I worked a lot on Websites and Databases, which helped me a lot on improving my Javascript and SQL Skills.

Transition to studying

Now I had a really good grasp of what I want to do in the future and where to start if I wanted to make further progress. I started a couple projects, like my first personal App or this Website. Not the mention the many ones who I did not finish or make public. I also was were interested in learning a lower level programing language like C++ or C, but since I was also doing an internship at the time I could only scratch on the surface of these subjects.

Softwareengineering and Managment - Bachelor Degree

My studies have not started yet but I cant wait for it. The last years I have invested a lot of time to learn by myself and hopefully guided lessons and a mentor will help my to answer the questions i could not answer myself. I also made an apllication for an internship at my university but i havent got an awnswer yet, so wish me luck!
