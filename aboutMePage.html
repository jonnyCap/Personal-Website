Hello!

I'm Jonathan, a future Softwaredeveloper!
I want to show you my rather short Coding journy so far and tell you a bit about my self...

Highscool

I originally attended a high school specialising in natural sciences based in Austria, Graz. Fortunatly we had a few opportunity adjust our school timetable and from the 5th grade on I could focus on Informatics. We could choose specific courses, allthough some were not voluntary. However, that's were i got my first insights into Webdevelopment, ECDL, Game-Programming, Network-technologie as well as Software- and Hardware technologie.

Transition to Armed Forces

I apllied for a special IT-Eduction at the Armed Forces where only very few people got accepted. Thankfully i got accepted but since i head only little experience I felt like I had to prepare myself. In the course of my preperation I wanted to start learning how to code properly. So far I only programmed in a Programming-Language called "Programming" which is a JavaScript-API and wanted try out something quit different. So I got into Java, learned basic as well as advanced concepts and from there on I just programmed as much as possible to improve my Skills.

Austrian Armed Forces

With my solid 19 years I had to join the Austrian Armed Forces to complete my basic military service. As mentioned before, I was selected to be one of the few austrian "Cyber-Soldiers". At first we got special education which took 2 Month. During this time we firstly learned a lot about the military, its morals, traditions and so on. And secondly we got insights into subjects like Cryptography or the safe-military-network. Afterwards we were spread all over Austria again und worked practically for 4 Month. In that time I worked a lot on Websites and Databases, which helped me a lot on improving my Javascript and SQL Skills.

Transition to studying

Now I had a really good grasp of what I want to do in the future and where to start if I wanted to make further progress. I started a couple projects, like my first personal App or this Website. Not the mention the many ones who I did not finish or make public. I also was were interested in learning a lower level programing language like C++ or C, but since I was also doing an internship at the time I could only scratch on the surface of these subjects.

Softwareengineering and Managment - Bachelor Degree

My studies have not started yet but I cant wait for it. The last years I have invested a lot of time to learn by myself and hopefully guided lessons and a mentor will help my to answer the questions i could not answer myself. Team Creator

I created an App with Android Studio and Java,
which allows you to create fair Teams!

Project Idea

I originally came up with the Ideo of programming Games. However, after I started a few attempts of creating Games in Unity with C#, I thought that at the time I would probably profit more from learning Android Studios first. Since I did not have any experience of that field of work I just wanted to do a simple App and started planning.

Planning

Coming up with an creative and not completly overdone Ideo was acutally way harder than expected. Especially rather simple Ideas were to find everywhere and I could not come up with anything new. But after a few days of thinking and casually living my Life, it clicked. A friendgroup of mine was playing volleyball a lot and we always had issues making teams. Noone really wanted to choose who they want to play with so an App would be optimal to solve that problem for us. So all I wanted was a simple App that creates a specific amount of faier Teams. So I started Planning and writing down everything on paper first...

Learning Android Studio

Tha planning stage was done really quickly, since the app was not complex at all and I had a pretty clear Idea what it should look like. Everything that had to be done now was to convert my idea to an actuall app and to do so I decided to use Android Studio in Combination with Java. I used Youtube as my main source and for a couple of days I just started Testapps to test out the features and learn the core functions. Allthough I felt like there was still a lot to learn, I decided to start with my App and continue my learning Journy by mainly doing.

Finishing the App

Despite the only few functions my app had, it took me quiet a while to finish it. Especially at the end a lot of fine tuning had to be done which took a lot of time. In the end of the day it was absolutly worth it however, since it was such a satisfaction. Here are some preview pictures of it:

I setup my Google Player Store Account, payed the fee, uploaded my App, which I than had to rework an additional 5 times because some requirements were not met and finally provide a ton of information. JS Mini-Games

A little and honestly really crappy Javascript-Game-Collection!
I designed and programmed these Games during my time at the Austrian Armed Forces and they honestly helped my a lot ot understand the programming language.
I will release these Games in an other repository.

Snake

This was the first Game I programmed with Javascript. At the time I was completle unfamiliar with the language and thats the reason why I did not use anything like Canvas or other mostly helping stuff. I simply used a grid filled with colored Divs to display the background as well as the snakes Body. Every tail-Div is saved in an array and all that happens is a interval which calls a function every 10 milliseconds to draw everything black except the divs saved in the array.

Flappy Birds

This Game is where I wanted to start experiencing with a randomly created Sourrding, Object-collision and gravity. I did not completly finish this Game however and the gravitational forces and the graphics felt rather unsatisfying to be honest.

Builder

Builder allowd you to place a view Divs with given forms whereever on the screen. Then you could pick them up and replace them somewhere else. This game helped in particular to get a sense of the relative locations of each divs and how to combinate that with the mouse-X and mouse-Y position. This was rather a test tho and could not be called a real game.

World Runner

This game is the first game created with HTML canvas but there is not much I want to show here because there is defenitly going to be a completly reworked version released of this Game.This is the only Game on here I acutally put some thought and effort into it. The simple Idea: You runt the world. Thats all I am going to say so far:). If you are interested in News about it you can sign up for News in the Footer!
